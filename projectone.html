About Project

This project focuses on designing a clinic appointment application aimed at enhancing user convenience. By understanding the needs of patients and the challenges involved in the appointment scheduling process, this application is designed to provide an efficient and user-friendly solution. Users can easily schedule, change, or cancel their appointments with just a few simple steps, as well as receive reminders to ensure they do not miss important meetings with healthcare providers.

Features include:

  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • User profile management
  • Access to medical records
  • Mockups designed in Balsamiq Mockup

Software Used

VS Code, My SQL, Balsamiq for wireframing.
